About us


Disasters are becoming more frequent and severe due to climate change, population growth, and urbanization. In order to improve disaster preparedness and risk reduction, educational efforts are crucial. The development of interactive web-based apps offers a promising approach to engage users and provide valuable information on disaster preparedness.


Despite numerous available resources online, many individuals, including children, have limited knowledge about disaster preparedness, making them less prepared for emergencies. Communities often have limited access to educational materials and resources focused on disaster preparedness. Furthermore, there is a significant gap in knowledge regarding the importance of disaster preparedness among people of all ages.


To address these challenges, the researchers proposed the development and implementation of “E-Safety,” an accessible e-learning platform that focuses on different disaster preparedness. The development of this e-learning platform offers a promising approach to engaging users and providing valuable information on disaster preparedness. It will explore the effectiveness and user engagement of an interactive educational web-based app for disaster preparedness. By providing the users with an accessible and engaging platform, “E-Safety,” intends to enhance users’ understanding, knowledge, and skills in disaster preparedness.
